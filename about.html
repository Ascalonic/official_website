About Ascalonic

Ascalonic is not a startup. Its not an Organization. Its not even a team!
So What are we? Its more like a codename we use whenever we play with technology or art.
Since it was founded in 2014, we never sticked to a formal company ideology. We looked around and we tried to do more. We created. And keeps on creating more.
We Develop desktop, mobile and web apps for other business. We design and develop products as our own. We constantly researches on building technology that may one day change the way we live. Over the course of 4 years, we have completed about 15 projects. We are still working on more.

Currently we are trying to harness blockchain techology and Artificial Intelligence to protect data and privacy instead of having it getting sold to some multi-billion-dolar-corporate!

As we have mentioned, we are a little less (or more) than a typical company. If you want to contribute to Ascalonic by funding it or by doing a project with us, feel free to. Our door is always open.
