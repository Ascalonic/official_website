Who are we?
Ascalonic Technologies

Ascalonic traces its roots back to 2007, as a little organization in Nirmala Public Scool called NCA - Nirmala Comedy Association, started by Varghese Mathai and Abhijith KA. Later, in 2010, Blackstar was born, for software development works. Later both were merged into NCABlackstar and continued the trend of Software Development.

Finally in 2014 It was renamed to Ascalonic. As of January 2019, Its a Private Limited Company, owned by Varghese Mathai and Nidhin Shaji

Ascalonic, thus has years of experience in the sector and we have focused on Creativity and Perfection when it came to the works we have done. We have tackled any form of challenge handed to us, and emerged victoriously in the end. Ascalonic is also known for its 100% value for money policy when it comes to outsourced works, on the contary to the ongoing trend of ultra-expensive software works
