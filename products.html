Piper Server is a free server-side web-application framework for .NET. It is a lightweight and easy to use alternative to ASP.NET. It's written in C# and can be referenced by any .NET application. You can setup a server within just 3 lines of code. API endpoints can be bound to methods you define. A detailed documentation is under preparation. Meanwhile you can download and give it a run.

Our Projects

Unified Students Portal - A students portal built for Innovate, By ACM Chapter of Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science. The portal can be used by the students to performs the actions which they have to stand in long queues to complete with normal procedure, all at their fingertips. Won the Best Software Solution Award


ZARS - or Zone Adaptive Response System is a module we developed with valuable Closed Contribution from Arun Baby and Eldhose Joy of MITS for Vehicles. The module uses cloud service to locate the traffic signs around them, or Long Range active RFID tags for the ones not on cloud, and displays them in a console in the vehicle. Won the Third prize at National level participating in ARM Design Challenge by ACCS and TVS at Anekal, Karnataka


Carbon - is a short film developed as Ascalonic Studio, a sci-fi film which focused on CGI Integration from the studio. Included performers from MITS for acting as well as on-set works. Shot in co-operation with GraceGraphics, Kolenchery.


Vigr - is a mobile application built for the prelims of CareHack hosted by CareStack (Good Methods Global). The application lets the patients search for doctors in a specific location by their specialization and then book appointments with them. The appointments are also added to Google Calender so that they won't miss out.


Barricade - was a system we developed as Ascalonic Black. It is essentially an 'offensive proxy' that has two end-points. One in a target computer with internet connection and another in your own. It acts as a browser equipped with proxy that fetches the response from the target computer. i.e., turns the target computer into your proxy server!


GENISYS - was basically our first work in Artificial Intelligence. An application developed for the event of same name part of Senseai2k16, intera-college techfest of MITS. It is a chat application that lets the contestants group chat. An AI bot fakes itself to be a human in the group. The contestants have to identify the bot by chatting each other using phrases!


ADCOM 2015 demo - 3D Animated video rendered in 3ds Max for ADCOM 2015 first round. The video featured a smart traffic light control system which senses ambulances and other emergency vehicles using RFID tags and favors their route so that they reach the destination without the hustle of traffic
